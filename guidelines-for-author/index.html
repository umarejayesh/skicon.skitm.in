Guidelines for Author

GUIDELINES FOR ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

Participants are most welcomed to submit their abstracts for poster presentation related to distinctive themes of Pharmaceutical Sciences: Pharmaceutical Technology, Medicinal Chemistry, Pharmaceutical Analysis, Quality Assurance, Pharmacognosy, Pharmaceutical Biotechnology, Phytochemistry, Pharmacology, Toxicology and Regulatory Affairs.

ABSTRACT CONTENT

The abstract of the work proposed to be presented should be typed according to the instructions given below. The abstracts should be typed only in the prescribed format.

  • TITLE OF THE ARTICLE:

Times New Roman, Font size: 14, Bold.

  • AUTHORS AND CO-AUTHORS:

Authors names (without such titles as Dr., Ph.D. etc.) and affiliations should be written below the title in the Times New Roman, using 12 font size. The presenting author name must be underlined. Maximum names should not exceed to 5.

  • ABSTRACT:

Abstract text should be typed in Times New Roman with 12 font size. The abstract should not be more than 250 words. Abstract must be headed under the headings: Introduction, Material and Methods, Result and Discussion and Conclusion.

  • FULL POSTER:

If paper is accepted, full content has to be presented under following heads in Times New Roman using a readable font size: Title, Objective, Experiments, Results & Discussions, Conclusion and Reference.

 

Abstract acceptance will be intimated through E-mail by 5th January, 2023.
